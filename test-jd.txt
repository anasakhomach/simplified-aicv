Job description
A Snapshot of Your Day

Join our dynamic Blades & Vanes Network Strategy team as a Data Scientist, where your expertise will drive data-driven forecast validation and manufacturing network capacity planning. You will analyze and visualize capacity gaps, empowering decision-making and enhancing planning efficiency. Collaborate with key partners across Procurement, Product Management, and Competence Centers to develop cross-organizational dashboards that steer the business with actionable insights. Imagine the impact you can make by introducing advanced analytics and machine learning solutions to solve complex network challenges!

How You'll Make an Impact

* Drive innovation by implementing advanced analytics and machine learning solutions to tackle complex network problems.
* Improve efficiency by optimizing the Sales & Operations Planning process, ensuring seamless operations.
* Empower decision-making through thorough data analysis, providing actionable insights that guide strategic choices.
* Identify trends and opportunities by analyzing large datasets, enabling proactive planning and resource allocation.

What You Bring

* A Master's degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Engineering, Business Administration, or a related field.
* Long-term technical expertise in Mendix, Snowflake, Alteryx, Tableau, or similar frameworks.
* Strong analytical skills with the ability to analyze complex datasets, identify patterns, and derive actionable insights.
* Innovative problem-solving skills and adaptability to learn new tools and techniques as the field evolves.
* Excellent communication skills to convey findings and recommendations to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Who is Siemens Energy?

At Siemens Energy, we are more than just an energy technology company. With ~100,000 dedicated employees in more than 90 countries, we develop the energy systems of the future, ensuring that the growing energy demand of the global community is met reliably and sustainably. The technologies created in our research departments and factories drive the energy transition and provide the base for one sixth of the world's electricity generation.

Our global team is committed to making sustainable, reliable, and affordable energy a reality by pushing the boundaries of what is possible. We uphold a 150-year legacy of innovation that encourages our search for people who will support our focus on decarbonization, new technologies, and energy transformation.
